Frequently asked questions

How do I perform a scan?

+

After logging in, go to the Settingssection. In Functionalities, complete the Credentials and IPspan forms. Turn on the API, enableMonitor Mode, and select the scan mode (Quick, Intermediate, or Deep). Quick Scan: Detects only basic services; it does not detect vulnerabilities or the operating system version. Intermediate: Detects the operating system version and more specific services but no vulnerabilities. Deep: Detects vulnerabilities, services, and the operating system. ensure your Raspberry Pi is properly configured.
